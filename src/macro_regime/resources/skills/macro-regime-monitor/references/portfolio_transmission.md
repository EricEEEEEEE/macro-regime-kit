# Portfolio Transmission

The kit reads `total_delta.beta_by_factor` and optional `exposures_usd`.

- `beta > 1` means the asset is high beta relative to the configured risk factor.
- If 30Y real yield breaks above 2.7%, review high-beta exposure first.
- Act 1 pressure usually hits high-beta growth and crypto.
- Act 2 needs external confirmation from auction stress and cross-asset selloff signals.
- Act 3 may be less hostile to hard assets if real yields are suppressed.
